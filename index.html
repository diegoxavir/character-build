Amuro Ray

Amuro Ray - The Pilot of the Gundam during the One Year War. After facing off against Char Aznable in the One Year War in UC 0079, and fighting along side him during the Gryps War, Amuro now see's himself facing off against Char once more. Now a Combat Squad Commander within the ranks of Lando Bell, Amuro With the aid of the RX-93 aka: Nu Gundam, Amuro hopes to save the earth by putting an end to Char and the Second Zeon War.

Noa Bright

After being a primary tactition during the One Year War, Gryps Conflict, and the first Neo Zeon War, Bright Noa has been appointed Leader of the Earth Federation Forece's Londo Bell Unit, and giving control of the Ra Cailum - Londo Bell's flagship. With the aid of Amuro Ray and allies made throught multiple conflicts and wars, Bright leads his crew to the fight against Neo Zeon.

Chan Agi

Chan is a technical officer for Londo Bell who helps Amuro supervise the development of his Nu Gundam during the Second Neo Zeon War.

Char Aznable

Char Aznable - Now leader of Neo Zeon, Char plans to bring a nuclear winter upon Earth, seeing it as the only way for New Types and Spacnoids to thrive. Having already dropping a space colony on Tibet, he comes one set closer to acheiving his goals. After fighting along side Amuro Ray during the Grypes War, Char now finds his Newtype rival Amuro once again as the only obsticle to his plans of Spacenoid absolution.

Quess Paraya

Daughter of the Vice-Foreign Minister of the Earth Federation, Quess herself holds no loyalty to the EFF or Earth itself. After tagging along the Ra Cailum, Quess eventually leaves the Londo Bell ship and aligns herself with Char Aznable. Letting Char utilize her Newtype abilities, Quess joins Neo Zeon in the Second Neo Zeon War.

Gyunei Guss

Gyunei is a Cyber Newtype, a form of Newtype originating from the Gryps Conflict. Gyunei finds himself as a high ranking officer in Char's Neo Zeon army. Gyunei's main goals don't seem to fully align with Neo Zeon's, but he'll fight the EFF's forces non the less.
